Taro Sakamoto

Taro Sakamoto (坂本さかもと太郎たろう Sakamoto Tarō) is the main protagonist of the series Sakamoto Days. Sakamoto was considered to be the best assassin in the world and a former member of the Order, an elite special unit of the JAA. He holds a fearsome reputation within the assassin world, that those who knew him fear him, and rever him for his prowess.

Upon retiring from being an assassin, he now runs a convenience store with his wife, Aoi, and daughter, Hana. After a bounty is placed on his head, Sakamoto is thrust back into the world of assassins, alongside his new allies Shin, Lu, and Heisuke.

Shin Asakura

Shin Asakura (朝倉あさくらシン Asakura Shin), also known as the Clairvoyant (千里眼の), is the deuteragonist of the series Sakamoto Days. Shin is an ex-hitman and an employee at the Sakamoto's Store that was once an old partner and former student of Taro Sakamoto. After failing to kill Sakamoto, Shin joins him and his family.

His name at birth would likely have been Shin Ando, however, at some point, he took that name of Asakura, going by Shin Asakura. However, all materials related to the JAA (his hitman license[3]) and JCC (his test results[4]) show just his given name, Shin.

Lu Shaotang

Lu Shaotang (陸ルー 少糖シャオタン Rū Shaotan, Lu Xiaotang by Viz) is the tritagonist in the Sakamoto Days series. She is the daughter of a Triad Family. She currently works alongside Shin as a store clerk at Sakamoto's and can be considered a part of the Sakamoto Family.

Heisuke Mashimo

Heisuke Mashimo (眞ま霜しも平へい助すけ Mashimo Heisuke) is a major supporting character in Sakamoto Days. He is a low-ranking assassin with a talent for sniping, but nothing else. Due to his lack of diversity in his skills, he struggled to find employment and was forced to live on top of a building in a severely damaged tent.

Heisuke owns a bird named Piisuke, whom he shares a close bond with.

Kei Usuki

Kei Uzuki (有う月づき憬けい Uzuki Kei), also known as "X" or "Slur" (☓スラー Surā?), is the main antagonist of the Sakamoto Days series. He is a serial killer seeking to overthrow the JAA with a group of fellow assassins. Roughly around the same time Taro Sakamoto was given his bounty, Uzuki slaughtered one-fourth of the assassin industry, leaving a large 'X' mark at the scene of his crimes written with the blood of his victims.

Gaku

Gaku (楽が) is one of the two secondary antagonists (alongside Kashima) of Sakamoto Days.

He was an assassin working with Slur and a member of X's Organization as he aids him in overthrowing the JAA. He was an orphan at the Al-Kamar Orphanage, a failed plan to create Order members.

Yoichi Nagumo

Yoichi Nagumo (南ナ雲グモ与ヨ市イチ Nagumo Yoichi) is a major supporting character in Sakamoto Days and an old friend of Taro Sakamoto and the late Rion Akao. He is first seen paying a visit to Sakamoto to inform him of his bounty [6] and was revealed to be a member of the Order before losing his seat during the Assassin Exhibition of the Century Arc.
